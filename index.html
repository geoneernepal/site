About Us

Geoneer Geospatial Service is a Geospatial based industry focusing company founded in 2021.

Driven towards Technological innovations using state-of-the-art technologies powerful medium in triggering change. When technological innovations intervene correctly, big changes can take place. Context-fitting solutions can often lead nations to great accomplishments in terms of better livelihood and sustainable development. While we continue in this collective effort of shaping the future, we aim at leading the geoinformatics domain.
