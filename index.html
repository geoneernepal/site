About Us

Geoneer Geospatial Service, an innovative and passionate geospatial based industry founded in 2022 with the focus on scouting diverse professional skills and identification of future geospatial strategies. Geoneer, the technological, ambitious and young company, was founded in 2022 by three youths from the Geomatics Engineering faculty of IOE (Institute of Engineering), Tribhuvan University, who have a keen interest in the geospatial sector. The company has an innovative motto to explore the geospatial industry through the integration of spatial ideas with digital technology.

Geoneer leads the geomatics industry in Nepal by combining evolving services like land surveying, aerial surveying, GIS and Remote Sensing, land use planning, land consolidation, DGPS surveying, House plan designing, forestry and many more environmental and geospatial solutions.
